What is Laois Arch Club?

Laois Arch Club is a friendship club for adults with intellectual disabilities. Founded in 1998, this local voluntary organisation relies totally on fundraising and donations to remain in existence. Membership of the club is open to anyone over the age of 16 with an intellectual disability and our members come from all parts of Laois, Offaly, and Kildare.

Laois Arch Club was set up to provide an opportunity for our members to meet up with friends in a safe and fun filled environment. We have a social night on the last Thursday of every month in The Midlands Park Hotel with our resident DJ making sure that everyone has a great night. We usually have between 60/70 members attending and the atmosphere is always electric!

Once a year the Arch club volunteers take a group of about 40 of our members away for a weekend and this is the highlight of the year. Not only does this weekend provide an exciting weekend away for members but its also provides a very well deserved break for their families. Members who do not get a chance to go on the weekend away are invited on a day trip. Both of these outings help foster new friendships with both members and our volunteers.
